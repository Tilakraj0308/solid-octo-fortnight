Cozen

Description

to deceive, win over, or induce to do something by artful coaxing and wheedling or shrewd trickery

Usage

  • The point, however, is not to entertain the driver/employee but to cozen the owner.
  • He cozened several elderly ladies into believing that he was intending marriage.
